Loading Photos

  1. Touch and hold on the image above, then select "Save Image."

  2. Then go to your Photos app, select the image and tap the arrow icon in the upper right.

  3. Finally, tap "Use as Wallpaper." (most of these photos could work as both your Lock and Home screen wallpaper)
